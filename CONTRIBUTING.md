# Contributing a Template

Thank you for helping other authors.

## Before submitting

- Test the template using Aetherpaloo's editable first-chapter preview.
- Confirm that descriptor, title, rule, alignment, spacing, and body typography behave correctly.
- Export through **Share with Community** so manuscript data is stripped.
- Include a screenshot that contains invented sample text or text you own.

## Privacy and copyright

A submission must not contain:

- manuscript text or chapter titles;
- author, book, ISBN, publisher, or sales metadata;
- cover art or embedded images;
- fonts, logos, or artwork you cannot redistribute.

## Submission process

1. Open a **Template submission** issue.
2. Attach the `.aethertemplate.json` file.
3. Attach one preview screenshot.
4. State the creator name and license.
5. A maintainer validates the schema and reviews the preview.
6. Accepted templates are added to `templates/` and `index.json`.

Submitting a template does not transfer ownership. You grant users the rights stated in the template's `license` field.
